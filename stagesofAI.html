AI in the past

For centuries, human anxiety has concerned the development of machines and systems that have a degree of intelligence in dealing with some problems and situations that a person faces in his daily life. Many scholars and philosophers in ancient times have worked to develop scientific and logical approaches in order to simulate human intelligence and ways to produce logical thinking that can solve problems based on logic sciences.
