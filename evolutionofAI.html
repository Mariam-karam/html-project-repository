intelligence (Al) is evolving- literally. Researchers have created software that borrows concepts from Darwinian evolution, including "survival of the fittest," to build Al programs that improve generation after generation without human input. The program replicated decades of Al research in a matter of days, and its designers think that one day, it could discover new approaches to Al.
